  • What is SELF-DEFENCE?

    Its about how to recognize a dangerous situations early and to recognize what type of dangerous situation it is, which gives you the clues as to how take care of the situation verbally and in your quickest and safest way

  • Why we started SELF-DEFENCE for women?

    SELF-DEFENCE opens up this source of freedom for women in an imperfect world. Building confidence in physical resistance skills allows women to feel more capable and more at peace when in potentaially dangerous areas at they know the proper techiques to help fend off an attacker.

  • Why we came up with the idea of SELF-DEFENCE for men?

    It's safe to say that most men have been in some kind of altercation at one time or another. In some ways, they may feel reluctant to talk to others about SELF-DEFENCE for men because of the social idea that they, as men are already supposed to be able to take care of themselves and their loved ones. But many of us, of course, did not grow up in the circumstances where we would actually learn to fight others. So, we don't actually know what to do and don't have confidence in our natural abilities. Thus men need to learn SELF-DEFENCE too! The male version of course is called Resisting Aggression with DEFENCE.
